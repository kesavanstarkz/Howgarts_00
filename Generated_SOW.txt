SCOPE OF SERVICE:  

The Portal will support the RDU Business operations team by consolidating all commercial information and reporting into a single location. This will allow the team to quickly identify information needed for business decisions without time consuming navigation between different platforms.  

Scope Items:
  • Development, Launch and Hypercare of the Application (25th Apr'24 - 31st July'24)
  • Operational Support of the Application as detailed in Section 16 (1st Aug'24 - 31st Dec'24)

Phase 1 of our project focuses on implementing core features such as single-sign-on authentication, collaboration through Microsoft Teams, favorites management, search capabilities, self-service options and ensuring accessibility across PC, iPad, and mobile devices. Additionally, an admin feature will be established to maintain user security and manage portal functionalities effectively. 

Phase 2 will bring in additional functionalities, including analytics via Chatbot powered by OpenAI ChatGPT.
